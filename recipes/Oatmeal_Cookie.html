Oatmeal Cookie

Description

Oatmeal cookies are a light and delicious snack that are pretty simple to make. You dont need the most experience needed when it comes to baking and it might only take 2 hours to make 24 cookies for everybody to enjoy. They are also a healthy alternative to other cookies. Now lets get on with what you will need to make these and how you will make them.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brown sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 3 cups quick cooking oats
