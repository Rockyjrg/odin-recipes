Lasagna

Description

A warm and cheesy delicious dinner that can feed many people, lasagna. We all love it or at least I know I do.

It is stacked pasta sheets with meat and cheese in between them.

You usually put it in the oven to melt all of the cheese and cook the pasta sheets making sure that the ground beef cooked before hand

Ingredients

  • 2 teaspoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 pound ground beef chunk
  • 1/2 medium onion, diced
  • 1/2 large bell pepper
  • 2 cloves garlic, mined
  • 1 can tamato sauce
  • 3 ounces tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh oregano
  • 1 tablespoon Italian seasoning
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 tablespoon red or white wine vinegar
  • 1 tablespoon to 1/4 cup sugar
  • Salt
  • 1/2 pound dry lasagna noodles
  • 15 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1 1/2 pounds grated mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 pound grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Put pasta water on to boil
  2. Brown the ground beef
  3. Cook the bell pepper, onions, garlic, add back the beef
  4. Transfer to medium sized pot, add tomoatoes and remaining sauce ingredients to build the sauce
  5. Boil and drain the lasagna noodles
  6. Heat the oven to 375 F
  7. Assemble the lasagna
  8. Bake
  9. Cool and serve
